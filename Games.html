Manual Instructions:

To begin the would need to or more players of your choice to be able to play.Set out a piece of paper and write down each of the players name to help you you keep in track of the scorce. After that's done one player would give a demistration on how the game works. The choosen player would show 3 figures that would be played in the game. The first figure would be in a fist for rock,the second figure would be a opened hand for paper, and the third would be a two fingers up for scissors.

To begin the game input your first response. RPS
